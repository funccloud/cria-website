About Cria

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become the leading parent-first platform for discovering and managing kids’ activities, expanding across cities and eventually to global markets.

Mission

To simplify parents’ lives by connecting them with high-quality extracurricular opportunities tailored to their children’s interests and needs, while empowering providers with intuitive tools.

Problem & Solution

Problem

Parents often struggle to find and organize extracurricular activities for their children. Discovery, scheduling, communication, and payments are fragmented.

Solution

Cria brings everything into one place:

  • Curated activity suggestions
  • One calendar for multiple children
  • In-app bookings and messaging
  • Tools for providers to manage and grow their business

Helping families discover meaningful activities for their children.
